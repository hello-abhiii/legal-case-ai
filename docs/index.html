About This Project
Academic Year 2026–2027 | Department of Information Retrieval & ML

This system assists legal professionals by leveraging Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing to analyze legal cases and provide data-driven insights.

🛠 Technology Stack
Python + FastAPI

📊 Model Performance

The model achieves 89.56% accuracy using 5-fold cross validation on 66 Indian court cases spanning 10 IPC sections including theft, cheating, assault, forgery, robbery and more.
